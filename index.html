The Global Holidays:

Our mission is the spread the world of every holiday we can.
One day at a time.

New Year's Day

Description:
The start of the new year, celebrated by almost every user of the Gregorian calendar.
Janurary 1st

Lunar New Year

Description:
The start of the year on Lunar Calendars, mostly celebrated by Asian countries.
Date varies

Easter

Description:
A Christian holiday which celebrates the resurrection of Jesus, but many ideas from it are used in non-religious places.
Date varies

Saint Patrick's Day

Description:
An Irish holiday, but celebrated in multiple places.
March 17th

April Fools

Description:
A holiday focused on tricking others and hoaxes, usually celebrated in western countries, but is also done in many other places too.
April 1st

Halloween

Description:
An originally Christian holiday, but now celebrated by many people, focused on giving out food and deceiving others.
October 31st

Valentine's Day

Description:
Originally a Christian holiday, but now a day focusing on giving gifts to loved ones.
February 14th

Christmas

Description:
Originally a Christian holiday, but now a day focusing on giving gifts to family and friends, and a unknown figure known as the "Santa Claus"
December 25th

Bread Day

Description:
The most important holiday, celebrated by all living beings, no matter if you are aware of it or not, do not try to resist, resistance is futile.
Every day.
